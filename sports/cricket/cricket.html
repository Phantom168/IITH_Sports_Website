Cricket

BANG!!!

A remarkable journey unfolds in the name of Cricket Club at IITH. One gets a chance to showcase their skills and represent IITH at the Inter-IIT level.

During the Even Semester, the Club organizes two different T20 Tournaments whereas during the Odd Semester you get an opportunity for a one to one tournament with BITS Hyderabad.

In addition to the sporting events, the Club also takes pride in fostering a sense of oneness and environment full of opportunities. We also mean to use sports as an instrument to instill collectiveness and togetherness among all members.
